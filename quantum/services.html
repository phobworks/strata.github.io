Geological Consulting and Services

Quantum Marine Limited collects and interprets data related to the earth’s surface, the soil and rocks beneath. On this basis, the company provides a range of geological base consulting and services designed to give our clients innovative solution to achieve their objectives in the most cost effective way, expositive and least disruptive manner. The following are our range of geological services.


Geophysical and geotechnical soil investigation (onshore, near-shore & offshore)

Hydro-geological assessments and study.

Insitu soil stress measurement.

Contaminated land site investigation.

Foundation and Flood defense engineering..

Slope stability and Soil stabilization.
