QUALITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The company has put in place a pool of highly competent QA/QC department manned by a senior QA/QC Officer that carries out checks on all the data acquire from the field, during office processing and the final product. All field sheets and field data are to be signed by the QA/QC officer who also carries out the check to ensure that all data are error free.

An Inspection / Quality Control Officer shall at all stages be present to ensure that the appropriate standards of quality are achieved and that tests are duly conducted where and when necessary.

Quantum Marine Limited will not compromise any level of quality in any part of stage of the works.

Pre-commissioning test will be carried out on every completed stage of the work. The entire electrical joint will be protected and insulated to avoid partial contact as well as electric shock. Pressure test will be carried out on all the plumbing work and the existing flow lines. Penetrant test shall be conducted for structural welds by the use of aerosol sprays in accordance with the manufacturer’s instructions.

All tests shall be non-destructive process. A pre-commissioning manual exists specifically for this purpose.

Client representative and commissioning Engineers shall be obliged to inspect this work scope. This scope amongst other matters, cover test of pump and other electrical equipment and connections, pipes and pipe joints, water treatment plant and storage facilities.


QUALITY ASSURANCE POLICY STATEMENT

It is the policy of Quantum Marine Limited to provide products and services which comply with contract specification.

It is the management’s intent and commitment to ensure a planned and organized quality system which can demonstrate execution of those products and service in accordance with ISO 9000 series and other equivalent international standards. We aim to meet or exceed customer’s requirement for performance, time scale, quality, competitive cost and service and we are committed to on-going improvement of our products and services by a process of employed participation and teamwork.

In order to achieve these quality objective the company places importance on the tracing and eradication of causes of error with emphasis on prevention thus achieving high quality and improved efficiency.

The Quality Manager has the responsibility of coordinating and implementing the Company’s Quality System: identifying quality related problems and recommending corrective actions to eliminate non-compliance.
