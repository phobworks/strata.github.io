About

Strata Base Properties Limited is a licensed company with a 100% indigenous holding. Strata Base Properties Limited was set up to provide a one stop shopping mall with product range covering all aspects of Engineering, Human and Logistics services tailored to meet specific and general requirements of the major players in the Nigerian economy with particular interest in the Oil and Gas sub-sector.To meet this daunting aspiration , the company keeps constantly updated data base on Human, material and equipment resources that might be required in the efficient execution of work packs. Additionally, Strata Base Properties Limited liaises with affiliate in different part of the world, America, Europe and Asia and they in turn provide vital support and assistance to Strata Base Properties Limited operations in Nigeria.
