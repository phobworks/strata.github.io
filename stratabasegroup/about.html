Strata Base Group

Strata Base Group is a privately owned Limited Liability Company comprising of Seven (6) major companies; Quantum Marine Limited, Strata Base Nigeria Limited, Strata Base Properties Limited, Strata Base Polo Team Limited, Qoorier Limited and Elchudim Ventures Limited. These companies have been involved in full fledge operation and consultancy services in the Oil & Gas, Power, Public, Media, Advertisement, Courier, Logistics, Construction and Sport/Recreational sector of the economy.

With our team of graduate scientists, engineers and renowned sport men coupled with wealth of theoretical knowledge and practical experiences, we are sure to deliver best in class services.

We look forward to a lasting and rewarding business relationship with your organization. And if further clarification be required, we will be pleased to furnish you with more details.
