About Us

welcome to Elchudim Ventures Limited

Elchudim Ventures Limited was incorporated in Nigeria with RC. No: 200192 on July 7th, 1999 as a privately owned Limited Liability Company and has since been involved in full fledge operation and consultancy services in Oil & Gas, Power, Public and construction sector of the economy.

With a talented and dedicated group of engineers and professionals, our diverse expertise enables us to design and implement a wide range of civil and structural engineering projects from conception to completion.

Our vision

To be a profitable key player in the Nigerian built environment by providing quality world class civil and structural engineering services

Our Objectives

Health and Safety

The primary objective in every project is to provide a risk free working environment for worker and clients representatives. That’s why Elchudim follows strict procedures in implementing HSE rules.

Quality Control Management

Elchudim Quality Control Management plan was designed with objectives ensuring the Client’s satisfaction with quality of both work achieved in the construction stage and with the finished product. Each project, regardless of size, has a dedicated Quality team to assess everything from the materials used to the method of construction to site cleanliness.

Community Services

Building partnerships for sustainable development is part of our company’s corporate philosophy over the years, Elchudim has seen itself as part of the people and environment in which it operated by identifying positively with their economical social and cultural needs and aspirations.
