About Us

STRATA BASE NIGERIA LIMITED was established in 2003, with a base office in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. The Company was registered as an Oil & Gas Servicing Company with emphasis on leasing of Marine and Land Equipment. With its office currently situated at #6a Premabiri Street, D/Line, Portharcourt, Rivers State. On inception, with a staff strength of just 5 personnel and a single 400 ton dump barge, the company has grown to a total strength of over 30 key personnel and over 20 different marine and land equipment, STRATA BASE NIGERIA LIMITED story has been one of Grass to Grace.

STRATA BASE NIGERIA LIMITED being fully NIPEX certified is registered with SPDC, NLNG, MORPOL ENGINEERING, DEPTHWIZE, INDORAMA, ADDAX, NPDC, NNPC GROUP, NAOC to mention a few.
